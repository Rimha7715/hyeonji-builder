About Asset Growth Insights

Empowering users with data visualization tools.

Our Mission

At Asset Growth Insights, we believe that complex data should be accessible and engaging. Our mission is to provide clear, visual comparisons of major financial assets to help users understand market trends over time.

The Data Behind the Charts

Our 12-Year Asset Growth Comparison tool uses historical price data to visualize the performance of Bitcoin, Gold, and the S&P 500 from 2014 to 2026. By indexing these assets to a starting value of 100, we provide a normalized view of how each has grown, allowing for a direct comparison of relative performance regardless of their unit price.
