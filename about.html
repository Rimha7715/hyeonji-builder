About Asset Growth Insights

Empowering users with data visualization and AI tools.

Our Mission

At Asset Growth Insights, we believe that complex data should be accessible and engaging. Our mission is to provide clear, visual comparisons of major financial assets to help users understand market trends over time. In addition to financial data, we explore the intersection of technology and entertainment through our AI-powered interactive tools.

The Data Behind the Charts

Our 10-Year Asset Growth Comparison tool uses historical price data to visualize the performance of Bitcoin, Gold, and the S&P 500. By indexing these assets to a starting value of 100, we provide a normalized view of how each has grown, allowing for a direct comparison of relative performance regardless of their unit price.

AI Technology

Our "Animal Face Test" utilizes Google's Teachable Machine technology. By leveraging pre-trained image recognition models, we provide a fun and interactive way for users to engage with artificial intelligence right in their browser. We prioritize user privacy, ensuring that all image processing is done locally on your device.
