Privacy Policy

Transparency and data protection are our priorities.

Introduction

Welcome to Asset Growth Insights. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Data Collection

We collect minimal information required to provide our services:

  • Contact Information: If you use our Partnership Inquiry form, we collect your email address and message to respond to your inquiry.
  • Usage Data: We may use cookies and similar technologies via third-party services like Google AdSense to analyze traffic and improve user experience.
  • AI Tool: Our Animal Face Test processes images locally in your browser. No image data is sent to our servers or stored by us.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense: Used to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits.
  • Disqus: Used for our community discussion and comments section.
  • Formspree: Used to handle our contact form submissions securely.

Google AdSense and the DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.
